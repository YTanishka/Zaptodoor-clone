Zaptodoor - Shipping Policy

Zaptodoor is committed to getting your essential items to you quickly and conveniently. Here's what you need to know about our shipping policy.with in a week

Order Processing

We strive to process orders within 1-3 business days of receiving them. During peak times or for high-demand products, there may be slight delays.

Shipping Rates

Shipping rates are calculated based on:

  • Order weight:The total weight of the items in your order.
  • Destination:Your delivery location.
  • Shipping method:The shipping method you choose at checkout.

We offer a variety of shipping methods to meet your needs, with options for different speeds and costs. You will see the available shipping methods and their corresponding rates at checkout before you confirm your order.

Delivery Times

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the chosen shipping method. You will see an estimated delivery time range at checkout based on your selected options.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email that includes a tracking number. You can use this number to track the progress of your delivery on the carrier's website.

Free Shipping

We may offer free shipping promotions from time to time. These promotions will be clearly indicated at checkout.

International Shipping

At this time, Zaptodoor does not offer international shipping.

Undelivered Packages

If your package is marked as delivered but you haven't received it, please contact us immediately. We will work with the carrier to investigate the issue.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our shipping policy, please contact us by phone at +91 9200018690 or email at info@zaptodoor.com.
